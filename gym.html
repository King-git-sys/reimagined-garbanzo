About Us

Our recreational classes strike the perfect balance between fun and skill-building. In these welcoming and exciting sessions, we teach the fundamentals of gymnastics to budding athletes. Whether your child is a cartwheel enthusiast or a budding gymnast, our classes are the right place to begin their gymnastics journey.

What we offer

  • Front Flips

    Also known as a front somersault, is a gymnastic or acrobatic movement where you rotate forward in the air and land on your feet.

  • Back Flips

    A back flip involves rotating backward in the air and landing on your feet.

  • Calisthenics

    Calisthenics is a form of exercise that uses body weight movements to build strength, flexibility, and coordination.

  • 360 Flips

    An acrobatic movement performed in gymnastics that involves rotating your body a full 360 degrees
